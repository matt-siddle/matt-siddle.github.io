Lovely chatting the other week - I've had a proper think about how a Supa Phat x Pride SA partnership could come together for the October show, and wanted to put the shape of it down for you.

The idea is simple: one consistent look across the whole show. The amateurs, the pros and the ring itself, all kitted in premium Supa Phat gear instead of a patchwork of borrowed brands. A card that looks unified and professional, corner to corner, on the canvas and on camera. Anchored by a Supa Phat x Pride shorts collab in your colours to mark the 10-year anniversary - worn by the pros on the night, and a genuine piece of the show's history.

I've mapped three ways we could partner, each one building on the last, so you can pick the level that feels right:

Foundation - kit the grassroots and brand the ring.
  • Amateur protective gear - shins, elbows, chest and head guards
  • Supa Phat shorts for the pros (the 10-yr anniversary collab, in Pride's colours)
  • Ring rope covers and hand wraps
  • Free team apparel - tees, jumpers and singlets
Contender - everything in Foundation, plus the pros fighting in our premium Supa Phat gloves.
Championship - the whole show in Supa Phat, ring to corner.
  • Everything in Contender
  • Supa Phat shorts for the amateurs too
  • Gloves committed across the full card
  • A Supa Phat ring canvas

We'll bring the gear and the brand muscle either way. Where I'd really love your thinking is on the other half of it, because this works best as Pride x Supa Phat, not "sponsored by".

I reckon it's worth grabbing a proper catch-up to talk it through, specifically:

  • What it looks like from Pride's side, and which level feels right for October
  • How the social side works - Supa Phat featured across your channels through the build-up and on the night, footage and moments we can share with our community, maybe a "presented by Supa Phat" sit-down with one of your fighters
  • Your ideas, and anything we're not thinking of - how you'd come to the table at each level, so it's genuinely good for both of us
