What Pursuit Is Known For

Muay Thai

$40 / week · adults & kids

Pursuit was built on Muay Thai. Founded in 2010 by 2x Middleweight World Champion Adam Bailey, the gym is part of the champion-producing Bailey Fight Team and coached by authentic Thai trainers. There's a structured grading pathway that takes you from your very first class to the ring — if that's ever where you want to go.

  • Beginner-friendly: technique, bag and pad work first — not thrown in to spar
  • Authentic Thai coaching with a real curriculum and grading system
  • Kids programs (Mini & Junior Morakots) — no head contact in kids Muay Thai
  • An invitation-only fight team for those who want to compete
